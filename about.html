Who We Are

Teknovis — Where Technology Meets Transformation

Teknovis Pvt Ltd is a Pune-based EdTech and AI solutions company with a singular mission: to equip professionals and enterprises with the technology capabilities needed to thrive in an AI-driven economy.

Founded by an industry veteran with deep roots in both enterprise technology and education, Teknovis bridges the persistent gap between academic learning and industry requirements. Our programs are designed by practitioners who work daily with the tools and frameworks that define modern technology careers.

On the enterprise side, we partner with businesses across sectors to design, build, and deploy AI-powered systems — from intelligent automation and data analytics platforms to generative AI applications. Every engagement is anchored in tangible business outcomes.

Meet Our Founder

Shilpa Honnannavar brings a rare combination of technical depth and pedagogical expertise to the leadership of Teknovis. Her career spans enterprise AI implementations, business intelligence architecture, and technology training — giving her an intimate understanding of both what industry demands and how individuals learn most effectively.

Having worked with organisations ranging from mid-sized enterprises to large corporates on data analytics and AI transformation initiatives, Shilpa identified a persistent frustration: talented professionals were unable to transition into high-value tech roles because available training was either too theoretical or too outdated to be genuinely useful.

That observation became the founding thesis of Teknovis. Every course, every service, every engagement at the company is designed through the lens of real-world applicability — ensuring that what learners study in the classroom is exactly what the job requires on day one.

Shilpa continues to actively mentor learners, lead client engagements, and shape the strategic direction of the company — remaining deeply connected to both the training and enterprise sides of the business she built.
